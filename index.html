The Beauty Standard.

In the age of social media, and the internet, there has become a more extensive beauty standard that subjects good hair to be straight hair, and when curly hair is represented only looser textures, are shown to be beautiful in mainstream media.

Natural hair has become more popular in the 2010s, but in the 2020’s has now has had a noticable shift in popularity.
