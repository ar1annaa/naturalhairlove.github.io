Be The Change!

Whether you are a person with curly hair, or not you can still be apart of the solution to this global topic.

Think Before You Speak

Be mindful in what you say intentionally/unintentionally about black girls hair.
Saying things like "Is that your REAL hair?", touching our hair without permission,
or unsolicted non-genuine comments are not okay.

Encourage Hair Love At A Young Age.

- A great way to encourage hair love is to expose young girls to it as soon as you can. Some ways to do this can be toys, books, and media consumption.

- Purchasing toys helps girls to become familar and used to seeing girls with hair just like them.

- Purchase books that teach the importance of hair love!

SELF love.

- A great way to help your self love journey, is affirmations to yourself.

-Speaking to yourself in the mirror each day saying how beautiful you are can really help you to believe what you speak, when done routinely.

- Another great thing to do is to experiment with diffrent hair products, and styles made specfically for your hair type

- Social media is a great tool, to use to find people with similar textures to you!
